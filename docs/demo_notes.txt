## Recording Tips
- Use **screen recording software** at 1080p resolution
- Keep mouse movements smooth and deliberate
- Pause 2-3 seconds after each major action for viewer comprehension
- Add **visual highlights** (circles/arrows) on Points/Set/Quantity during auto-calc demo
- Use **text overlays** to reinforce the formula and values
- Ensure audio is clear or add background music with text narration

---

## Recommended Recording Tools
- **OBS Studio** (free, open-source) - https://obsproject.com/
- **Camtasia** (paid, professional) - https://www.techsmith.com/video-editor.html
- **ScreenRec** (free, simple) - https://screenrec.com/
- **Windows Game Bar** (built-in, Win+G) - for quick recordings
